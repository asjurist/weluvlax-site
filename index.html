About WeLuvLax

A brand built from real pride in the girls’ game

Built with heart by a dad and coach of two daughters who have played the game from a young age and still do to this day, WeLuvLax is more than just a lacrosse apparel idea. It comes from wanting something that feels true to the girls’ game, something feminine, polished, supportive, and proud. Too much sports gear feels generic, overly aggressive, or disconnected from the actual culture around girls lacrosse. WeLuvLax is meant to feel different. It is for players who love the sport, for families who live on the sidelines and at tournaments, and for anyone who wants apparel and accessories that reflect the energy, pride, and style that surround the game. The goal is to create pieces that feel clean, elevated, giftable, and genuinely connected to girls lacrosse culture.
Feminine identity

A softer, more polished identity that feels true to girls lacrosse and does not reduce it to plain team gear.

Lacrosse first

Built around real girls lacrosse culture, the families around it, and the pride that comes from supporting daughters who play.

Preppy edge

Clean branding, easy layering, and a refined look that feels elevated enough for gifts, sidelines, tournaments, and everyday wear.
