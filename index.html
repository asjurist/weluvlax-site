Founder story

Built by a lacrosse dad. Inspired by the women’s game.

WeLuvLax comes from a real lacrosse family lifestyle — years of coaching, club ball, tournament travel, and living the rhythm of the game with my two daughters.

This brand grew out of love for the women’s side of the sport.

As a dad of two daughters who played lacrosse, I coached them through eighth grade, watched both of them compete in club, and spent years traveling to tournaments all over the country. Lacrosse was not just a sport for our family. It was our lifestyle.

What always stood out to me about the women’s game was the balance of finesse and power. There is beauty in the skill, speed, and control, but also real toughness in the athletes who play it. To me, that combination truly reflects the warriors on the field.

Because I only had daughters, that became my connection to the sport and the lens through which I came to love the game. When my youngest went off to college, there was a real void. WeLuvLax became a way to keep that connection alive and turn that love of the game into something lasting.
