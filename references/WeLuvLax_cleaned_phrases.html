About WeLuvLax

A brand built from real pride in the girls game.

Built with heart by a dad of daughters, WeLuvLax is more than just a lacrosse apparel idea. It comes from wanting something that feels true to the girls game and all that it represents. The feminine side. The finesse. The power. The discipline. The warrior spirit. Too much sports gear feels generic, overly aggressive, or disconnected from the actual culture around girls lacrosse. WeLuvLax is meant to feel different. It is for players who love the sport, for families who live on the sidelines and at tournaments, and for anyone who wants apparel and accessories that reflect the energy, pride, heart, and grace that surround the game.
